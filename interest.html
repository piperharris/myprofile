Interest in Information Technology

It is really hard to pinpoint when exactly my interest in IT started, as I have always been intrigued in anything technology related. In primary school I applied to be an ICT monitor and was selected due to my natural computer skills I had picked up from playing around. Numerous times whilst in high school, I put my name down to attend programming incursions and stem excursions which focused majorly on IT.
