Project Idea: [PROJECT NAME]

The idea for my project is to implement technology into a fridge which uses SOMETHING to sense what items are in the fridge and what need to be restocked on, sending an alert to a virtual shopping list. Having the technology determine what is needed to be purchased and adding it to the users shopping list, helps prevent them from forgetting a necessary item, or not knowing that they have ran out of something. The aim is to make grocery shopping easier by saving the user time, as they wouldn’t need to rummage around to see if they need something, or unknowingly have ran out of an everyday staple.

Many people have already started using lists on their smart devices to remember what they need to do or buy, so having technology which can automatically add to the list when something is missing in the fridge is a game changer. Not only will it save the user time, but it will also ensure that the user always has what they need. 99.8% of Americans own at least one fridge, meaning this technology could become very popular as it would be affecting and helping nearly everyone. I think this implementation would be very useful, as I know from personal experiences that everyone forgets an item when they are grocery shopping, because they forgot to write it down, or didn’t know they ran out of milk!
